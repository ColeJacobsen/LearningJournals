## Content Insights:
The two CMSs have similar structures and hierarchies to each other, but different ways of managing them. Both uses posts and pages, as well as using categories and tags for taxonomic purposes. They both are fairly easy to use but can have plenty of room for more complexity. I under stand how to create basic posts with both CMSs, but I could still learn how to integrate images/video, as well as colors and more complex designs.
