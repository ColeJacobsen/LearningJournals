## Content Insights:
Joomla! is based around articles, pieces of content consisting of HTML text and possibly links to more content, such as images or other sites. Every article is in one category based on the type of content contained. 
WordPress is based around a system of posts, pages, and media elements. 
Pages are found by clicking on links and are not directly connected to any other pieces/categories of content. 
Posts are smaller pieces of information that can be embedded into pages. Posts can possess different layouts, formats, and belong to a category based on the type of content. 
Media elements can either be uploaded during the creation of a post/page or created on their own. Includes images and videos and can be reused on different pages/posts. 
Pages and posts both use a block system, in which each piece of content on a post/page is stored inside its own block. The blocks can have different attributes (ex: position, alignment, color, font, etc) as well as types similar to HTML tags (heading, paragraph, etc)
