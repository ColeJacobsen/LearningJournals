## Content Insights:
For the prac this week, I will recount an experiment I conducted a while back. 
In this experiment, I tested what kind of solution would best dissolve a Tootsie Pop. PH neutral(water), acidic, or basic. 
Hypothesis: The acidic solution will dissolve it the fastest. 
Experiment: 
Take 3 Tootsie Pops of matching flavors.
Cut off as much of their sticks as possible. 
Individually weight each of the Tootsie Pops and record their weight. 
In separate containers, pour an equal amount of acid, basic solution, and water. 
Place 1 Tootsie Pop in each and wait 30 minutes. 
After 30 minutes, retrieve each Tootsie Pop and reweigh them and record the new mass. 
Calculate the dissolved mass of each. 
Result: The solution that caused the greatest change in mass is the most effective. In my experience, it was the basic solution.
